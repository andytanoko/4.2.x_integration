Title: GWFBase

        This module consists of entities required for BPSS and XPDL definitions to deploy into
    database.

    1)  Bpss entities are present in com.gridnode.pdip.base.gwfbase.bpss.model package and the ejb's
        for that entities are present in com.gridnode.pdip.base.gwfbase.bpss.ejb package.

    2)  Xpdl entities are present in com.gridnode.pdip.base.gwfbase.xpdl.model package and the ejb's
        for that entities are present in com.gridnode.pdip.base.gwfbase.xpdl.ejb package.
