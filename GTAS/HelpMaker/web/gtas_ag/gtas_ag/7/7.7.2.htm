7.7.2 To cancel the schedule certificate swapping for Own/Partner certificate

Step 1. Click on the Communication hyperlink. This will bring you to the Communication left panel where you can select and click Certificates. The Certificates page is displayed with the list of imported Partner and Own certificates.

Step 2. Edit the certificate from the list table of imported certificates. The browser brings you to edit the certificate. Make sure that the selected certificate is configured with a replacement certificate.

Step 3. Empty the date and the time in the “Swap Date” and “Swap Time” text box respectively.

Step 4. Click Save Imported Certificate. The scheduled certificate swapping for this particular certificate will be cancelled.
