7.4 Configuring Packaging Profiles

A packaging profile is a set of properties that determines how the documents in the business process are enveloped and packaged based on a channel as configured by a user.

You can modify your own packaging profile and profiles of partners.

Clicking Packaging Profiles will bring you to the All Packaging Profiles page where you can view a list of all packaging profiles in the detailed table. If you wish to view your own packaging profiles, click Own. Otherwise click Partner to view a list of partner packaging profiles.
