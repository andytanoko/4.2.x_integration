12.1.6 To schedule a weekly job

Weekly jobs are scheduled by choosing “Weekly” for the Frequency field. Additional input required:

Field

Explanation

Every n Weeks

To schedule jobs to run every N number of weeks.

On Days

Run the jobs on the days of the week, Monday - Sunday

For example, the scheduled task as shown in this figure will run jobs on 25th March 2004 12:00 noon, 14th June 2004 12:00 noon, 17th June 2004 12:00 noon, 6th September 2004 12:00 noon, 9th September 2004 12:00 noon, and so on, for the lifetime of the schedule setting.
