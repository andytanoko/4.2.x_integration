12.1.5.1 Type 1 Schedule

Additional input required for Type 1 schedule:

Field

Explanation

Every Day nth of Month

To schedule jobs to run on the Nth day of the month.

For example, the scheduled task as shown in this figure will run jobs on 15th Apr 2004 12:00 noon, 15th May 2004 12:00 noon, 15th June 2004 12:00 noon, and so on, for the lifetime of the schedule setting.
