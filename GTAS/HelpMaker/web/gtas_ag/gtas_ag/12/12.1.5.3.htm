12.1.5.3 Type 3 Schedule

Additional input required for Type 3 schedule:

Field

Explanation

Every nth Week of Month

To schedule jobs to run on the Nth Week of the month: First, Second, Third, Fourth, or Last

On Day

To run jobs on the day of the week, Monday - Sunday

For example, the scheduled task as shown in this figure will run jobs on 28th March 2004 12:00 noon, 25th April 2004 12:00 noon, 30th May 2004 12:00 noon, and so on, for the lifetime of the schedule setting.
