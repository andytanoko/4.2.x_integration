12.1.5.2 Type 2 Schedule

Additional input required for Type 2 schedule:

Field

Explanation

Every Last Day nth of Month

To schedule jobs to run on the last Nth day of the month.

For example, the scheduled task as shown in this figure will run jobs on 31st March 2004 12:00 noon, 30th April 2004 12:00 noon, 31st May 2004 12:00 noon, and so on, for the lifetime of the schedule setting.
