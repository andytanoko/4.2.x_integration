12.1.3 To schedule an hourly job

Hourly jobs are scheduled by choosing “Hourly” for the Frequency field.

Additional input required:

Field

Explanation

Every n Hours

To schedule jobs to run every N number of hours.

For example, the scheduled task as shown in this figure will run a total of 12 jobs on 24th March 2004 12:00 noon, 25th March 2004 00:00 midnight, 25th March 2004 12:00 noon, and so on, until the last job on 30th March 2004 00:00 midnight.
