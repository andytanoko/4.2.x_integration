16.1.3 To get your public certificate for distribution

Step 1. Go to Communication.

Step 2. Click Own under Certificate folder. This will bring you to the Own Certificates list view page where you should have already imported your private certificate.

Step 3. Follow Step 2 through Step 4 of Chapter 7.5 on “To download and install the Imported Own/Partner Certificate” to download you public certificate file.

Step 4. Distribute the downloaded certificate file to your partner.
