16.1.4 To import partner’s certificate

Step 1. Go to Communication.

Step 2. Follow Step 2 through Step 6 of Chapter 7.5 on “To Import Own/Partner Certificate” to import the partner’s certificate. Note: you should have your partner’s digital public certificate file at hand to perform this step.

Step 3. Click Partner under Certificate folder. This will bring you to the Partner Certificates list view page where the imported partner’s certificate is shown.

Step 4. Click the checkbox against the partner’s certificate record and click Export to Trust Store. This will insert the partner’s certificate to the trusted certificate store for outgoing HTTPs connections requiring server authentication. Transactions with the partner may start immediately without any restart of application.
