3.1 Renewing Expired License

Step 1. After login, you will be prompted to renew the license. Alternatively, you may go to the Registration feature. You will see the Register Product page is displayed indicating that your “GridTalk license has expired. Please update your license.”

Step 2. To select the new license file, click Browse. Then click Register.

Step 3. After a few moments, if the new license file is valid, your will see that the Registration State changes to “Registered”, and the details of the new license displayed accordingly.
