9.2.4 To trigger a partner function ranked level 4

To create a level 4 trigger:

Step 1. Click Triggers under the Process folder.

Step 2. Click Level 4.

Step 3. In the Level 4 Triggers list view page, click Create Trigger.

Step 4. In the Trigger detail view page, insert the necessary fields. Some fields that are highlighted blue are mandatory. For example, Partner Function, Document Type etc.

Step 5. Click Save Trigger. The newly created trigger will be displayed in the Level 4 Triggers and All Triggers list view pages.

Step 6. To edit the existing or current trigger, select the trigger and click on the Edit button . Update the entries as necessary. Click Save Trigger.

Step 7. To delete the existing or current trigger, select the check box of the trigger you want to delete. Click Delete Triggers. The selected trigger will be removed from the Level 4 Triggers and All Triggers list view pages.
