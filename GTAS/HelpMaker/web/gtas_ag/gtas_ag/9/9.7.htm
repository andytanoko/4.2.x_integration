9.7 Configuring Ports

The ports are used by GridTalk to locate the destination for exported files and to carry out RFCs when processing a partner function. The configuration for a port depends on whether you have back-end systems such as SAP, ERP, etc that you want to integrate with GridTalk. If you do, you will also probably have to define RFCs to configure the port.

There are two ways of configuring a port.

· Create a port using an RFC to communicate with backend systems

· Create a port without an RFC to communicate with another GridTalk
