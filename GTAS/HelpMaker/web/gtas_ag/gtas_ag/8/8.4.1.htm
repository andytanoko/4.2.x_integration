8.4.1 To create a new Business Certificate Mapping

Step 1. Click Certificate Mapping under the Mapping folder. The Business Certificate Mappings list view page is displayed.

Step 2. To create a new business certificate mapping, click Create Mapping. The Business Certificate Mapping detail view page appears.

Step 3. Select Partner. If the partner is not listed, click button to create a new partner.

Step 4. Select Partner Certificate. If this certificate is not listed, click button to import a partner certificate. For importing Partner Certificate, see Chapter 7.5.

Step 5. Select Own Certificate. If this certificate is not listed, click button to Import own certificate. For importing Own Certificate, also see Chapter 7.5.

Step 6. Click Save Business Certificate Mapping.

Step 7. To edit the certificate mapping, select the certificate mapping and then click on the corresponding Edit button . The screen appears. Update the entries of the certificate mapping as necessary. Click Save Business Certificate Mapping.

Step 8. To delete the certificate mapping, tick the Select checkbox of the certificate mapping you want to delete. As you see a mark appear in that checkbox, click Delete Mappings. The message box, “Are you sure you wish to delete these Mappings?” will appear. To confirm the deletion, click OK. The selected mapping will be removed from the Business Certificate Mappings page.
