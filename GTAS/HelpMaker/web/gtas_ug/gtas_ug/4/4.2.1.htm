4.2.1 To view a list of all business entities

Click Business Entities. The browser will bring you to the All Business Entities panel where you should see a list of own and partner business entities that have been earlier created. The following columns in the header of the All Business Entities table are displayed:

Edit:

This field allows you to update the details of your own or partner business entity.

Id:

This field identifies the Business Entity.

Enterprise Id:

This field is actually your own GridNode ID. If it is not specified, it means the business entity belongs to a partner.

Description:

This field describes the purpose of your own or partner Business Entity.

State:

This field indicates the state of your own or partner Business Entity.

Partner:

If the partner checkbox is ticked, it means that the business entity will be in the Partner Business Entity list itself. Otherwise it will reside in the Own Business Entity list.
