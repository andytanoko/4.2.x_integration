5.3.1 To create a partner group

Step 1. Click Partner Groups under the Partner folder. The Partner Groups list view page is displayed.

Step 2. To create a new partner group, click Create Partner Group. The Partner Group detail view page is displayed showing the empty fields.

Step 3. Enter the mandatory fields such as Partner Group and Description.

Step 4. Select the partner type using the drop down arrow button next to the Partner Type field. If the partner type you want to select is not found in the list, click on the icon to create a new partner type. (Refer to Chapter 5.2 for creating a partner type).

Step 5. Click Save Partner Group. The new partner group will be displayed in the Partner Groups list table.

Step 6. To add more partner groups, repeat the whole procedure. If you want to view the partner group, go to the Partner Groups page where you will see the records listed in the table. Click on the selected partner group’s hyperlink under Partner Group in the table. If you wish to update the selected partner group, click Edit Partner Group.

Step 7. To edit the existing or current partner group, select the partner group and then click on the corresponding Edit button . The Partner Group page is displayed. In the Partner Group page, update the entry of the partner as necessary. Then click Save Partner Group.

Step 8. To delete the existing or current partner group, tick the blank Select checkbox of the partner group you want to delete. As the mark appears in the checkbox of the highlighted partner type, click Delete Partner Groups. You will be prompted with the message box asking, “Are you sure you wish to delete these Partner Groups?” Then click OK. The selected partner group will be removed from the Partner Groups list table
