About CrimsonLogic eTrade Services

CrimsonLogic eTrade Services is a wholly-owned subsidiary of CrimsonLogic Pte Ltd. Established with the mission to serve the trade and logistics sector, we offer innovative and secure eTrade services to facilitate businesses to complete critical trade documentation and communication quickly and accurately. We enable businesses to connect and collaborate with their global trading partners seamlessly in a secure environment.

We have developed a suite of products under TradePalette®. TradePalette® is an end-to-end electronic trade management solution that enables the exchange and sharing of information for seamless business communications. It facilitates businesses in their business-to-business (B2B) and business-to-government (B2G) trade communication, coordination and collaboration, resulting in significant cost and time savings.

Our other core business is TradeWeb™ which streamlines the cargo declaration process by enabling users to send declarations to the Singapore Customs, IE Singapore, and other controlling agencies directly from the computer. The whole process of launching a cargo declaration to printing of a Cargo Clearance Permit (CCP) can be completed within minutes through the use of Trade Declaration services.

With a call centre in operation 24x7, our dedicated helpdesk serves as the business support centre for customer enquiries and troubleshooting. Our data centre infrastructure provides disaster recovery services to ensure that our customers’ data is protected and can be retrieved quickly to resume operations.
